About

ICT (Information and Communications Technology) is the integration of computing, telecommunications, and digital technologies that enable the creation, storage, transmission, and management of information. Definition and Scope ICT is a broad term that extends beyond traditional Information Technology (IT) by emphasizing communication and connectivity. It includes hardware such as computers, smartphones, tablets, servers, and networking devices; software like operating systems and applications; and telecommunications infrastructure including the internet, wireless networks, and satellite systems. ICT also covers audiovisual technologies, video conferencing tools, and distance learning platforms, enabling users to access, store, transmit, and manipulate information efficiently.
